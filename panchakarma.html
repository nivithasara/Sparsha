PANCHAKARMA

The ultimate in Mind-Body Healing & detoxification. Panchakarma literally means 5 actions. They are vamana, virechana, vasti, nasya and rakthamokshana.

Panchakarma is a cleansing and rejuvenating program for the body, mind and consciousness. It is know for its beneficial effects on overall health, wellness and self healing. It helps to eliminate toxins and toxic condition from our body and mind, and thus restore our constitutional balance improving health and wellness.

Dhara:- Stands as an important therapeutic procedure in diseases like insomnia, stress, migraine, insanity etc. Dhara is a main or supplementary procedure of pouring a liquid on forehead or all over the body or on a specific part of a body in a particular rhythm for a stipulated period of time. Medicated oils, herbal decoctions, milk, medicated butter milk etc are selected as dhara material according to the nature of the disease

Pizhichil:-pizhichil literally means squeezing.Here warm medicated oil isrepeatedly poured over the patient's body for a particular time period.

Udwarthanam:-is the procedure of massaging the body with herbal powders.It is found to be useful in obesity,diseases of neurological origin,skin diseases etc.

Abhyangam:-the most convinient oleation therapy which when done along with fomentation gives much effect in stiffness and pain of the body.

Tharpanam:-Is used in eye diseases like macular degeneration,computer eye strain, drooping of eyelid etc .Tharpanam is the procedure of pouring medicated ghee over the eyes and reataining it for a particular time period.
