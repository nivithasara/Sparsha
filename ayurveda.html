AYURVEDA

Ayurveda is a holistic medical system that has it origin in the vedic culture. Ayur mean life and veda means knowledge. According to sushrutha the two main objectives of ayurveda are

  • 1. The maintain the health of the healthy individual.
  • 2. To treat the disease of the diseased.

Ayurvedic Definition Of Health

" Samadosha Samagnischa
Samadhathumalakriyaha
Prasanna atmendriyamanaha
Swastha Ityadhidheeyathe "

ie, Balanced energies , balanced state of digestive fire , balanced tissues and excretions, peace of soul, sensed and mind. All these constitute health...[ Sushrutha Samhitha ]
